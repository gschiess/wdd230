PARC - Contrast

Unknown Author

https://xyproblem.info/

The xyproblem website is a great example when talking about the C in the PARC design principles. Although it is a very simple website I believe that helps when explaining how to use contrast to your benefit. The majority of the website is black text on a white background which helps for reading, but there are also spots where they use black on gray which helps grab your attention.

PARC - Repetition

BYU-Idaho

http://byui.instructure.com

The BYU-Idaho instructure website (on the card view), show a great example of the Repetition principle with how they repeat the cards used for the different classes. It helps keep the entire website organized, and showcase the different classes you have without thinking needed.

White Space and Clean Design

Monkeytype

https://monkeytype.com/

The monkeytype website is a good place to showcase whitespace and clean design. There is a good amount of stuff going on on this page but when you take a closer look its has a good design and has a good amount of padding in the certain areas where it needs its, such as all the social links in the footer. Also the white space in-between each seperate section gives it a clean design which makes it not look cluttered with the amount of stuff on the page.
