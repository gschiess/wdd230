Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve the local Chamber of Commerce in the Land of Point. It will serve as a meeting space and as a spot where local businesses can be showcased in order to build a healthy economy within the community. It will also server as a registry of sorts and will keep track of the local businesses in the area, which will help community members if they are searching for certain products or also other businesses if they are searching for partners in business.
